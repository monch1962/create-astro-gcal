# Astro Calendar Generator - Calendar Descriptions

This document explains the contents of each `.ics` file generated by the application.

## 1. Eclipses
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Solar_Eclipses.ics`**: Contains all Solar Eclipses (Partial, Annular, Total). Events show the maximum eclipse time.
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Lunar_Eclipses.ics`**: Contains all Lunar Eclipses (Penumbral, Partial, Total). Events show the maximum eclipse time.

## 2. Planetary Almanac (Rise/Set)
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_[Planet]_Almanac.ics`** (e.g., `Astro_Mars_Almanac.ics`):
    *   Daily Rise, Set, and Transit (Culmination) times for the specific planet.
    *   Calculated for the configured `OBSERVER_CITY`.
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Sun_Divisions.ics`**:
    *   Special breakdown of the Solar day (Sunrise, Sunset, Dawn, Dusk, Solar Noon).

## 3. Planetary Aspects (Geocentric & Heliocentric)
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_[Planet]_Geo.ics`** (e.g., `Astro_Venus_Geo.ics`):
    *   Geocentric aspects (viewed from Earth).
    *   Includes Exact Conjunctions, Squares, Trines, Oppositions, Sextiles, etc. involving the named planet.
    *   Event description includes the exact aspect angle and the other participating body.
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_[Planet]_Helio.ics`**:
    *   Heliocentric aspects (viewed from the Sun).
    *   Useful for analyzing planetary alignment independent of Earth's position.

## 4. Retrograde Motion
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Retrograde_Geo.ics`**:
    *   Events marking the **Stationary Retrograde** (start of backward motion) and **Stationary Direct** (end of backward motion) points for all tracked planets.

## 5. Moon Calendars
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Moon_Phases.ics`**:
    *   Exact times of New Moon, First Quarter, Full Moon, and Last Quarter.
    *   Description includes the zodiac sign of the Moon at that moment.
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Moon_Features.ics`**:
    *   **Perigee**: Moon closest to Earth.
    *   **Apogee**: Moon farthest from Earth.
    *   **Nodes**: Moon crossing the Ecliptic (Ascending/Descending Nodes).
    *   **Extremes**: Maximum North/South declination (Lunar Standstills).

## 6. Seasonal & Solar Events
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Seasons.ics`**:
    *   Exact times of the Equinoxes (Vernal/Autumnal) and Solstices (Summer/Winter).
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Solar_Year_Progress.ics`**:
    *   Milestones marking 0% to 100% of the *Solar* Year (based on Solstices).

## 7. Zodiac Ingress
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Zodiac_Ingress.ics`**:
    *   Events marking the moment a planet enters a new Zodiac Sign (e.g., "Mars enters Taurus").
    *   Includes the Sun entering signs (which corresponds to astrological calendar months).

## 8. Progress Calendars
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Calendar_Year_Progress.ics`**:
    *   Milestones marking 0% to 100% of the standard Gregorian Calendar year.

## 9. Special Patterns
*   **`[Prefix]_Astro_Square_and_Trine.ics`**:
    *   Rare occurrences where a planet forms a Square (90°) with one body and a Trine (120°) with another body simultaneously (within a specific time overlap).
